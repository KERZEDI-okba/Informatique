Brief description of my specialty
Informatics major

It is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves the design, development and analysis of software and hardware used in computing. Computer science is a highly interdisciplinary field that draws on concepts from mathematics, engineering, and other fields. Students who study computer science typically learn how to program, analyze algorithms, design and build computer systems, and work with databases. They also gain a deep understanding of computer architecture and computer networks. The field is constantly evolving, and students need to keep up with the latest technologies and programming languages.
